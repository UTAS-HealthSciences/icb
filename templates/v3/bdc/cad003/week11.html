Lecture

 
 

This week's lecture is about supporting your discussion using information presented in a visual form. You will be expected to be able to describe and interpret information presented in various visual forms. Indeed, you may be required in other units to be able to produce your own visuals and it is important that you are able to do this in an appropriate and meaningful way. Academic writing requires the writer to not only present facts but to use such information to support the given discussion and to enhance the reader's understanding.
