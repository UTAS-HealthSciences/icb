To access your collaborate session in real time

About 48 hours prior to our first session:

Work through the preparing for your first session PDF (or paste the following link into your browser - http://www.teaching-learning.utas.edu.au/__data/assets/pdf_file/0011/60221/Preparing-for-yourr-first-session.pdf ). It contains information about choosing audio equipment, setting up your computer for a web conference and accessing a web room. It also contains a brief overview of some of the main communication tools used in Collaborate and details of who to contact for support.

Access these sessions or the recordings, you need to go to the online room. You are not required to come to the session - they are recorded - and that if you do, you may log on and off at any time during the session. Here is a video showing how to access Collaborate sessions;

Collaborate online rooms - (streaming)

Collaborate online rooms - (download)

At least 15 minutes before our session is due to commence:

When you enter the web room:Set up your audio using the Audio Setup Wizard. You can launch the Wizard when in the Collaborate window by clicking on the Tools menu, then selecting Audio and Audio Setup Wizard. Follow the prompts to ensure that your microphone and speakers are working.

If you experience difficulties setting up your computer or accessing the web room: Visit the Web Conferencing Support for Participants web page (or paste the following link into your browser - http://www.teaching-learning.utas.edu.au/elearning/web-conferencing/support-students).
