This module

This module we are continuing working on really getting comfortable with using MyLO and on your emerging skills with paragraph writing. The reading for this week is the remainder of the first chapter of Cottrell (2013) and deals with aspects such as managing life as a student, managing anxieties and resources, the student experience, and resources available to you. It is important to complete the activities presented in this chapter. The feedback I have received from students about this text has been overwhelmingly positive and in fact a number stated that this text answered a number of questions they had regarding university study. By week three you will be expected to have your own copy of the Cottrell text (details of this are in the unit outline) and if you had ordered it in week one it should be arriving to you in time.

This week I will be focusing on linking words, avoiding repetition, an introduction to discursive essays, and some more linking words and phrases.

Discussion

 
 
As a result of what you have read in the reading this week (Cottrell pp 24-34), begin to make another network of peers online. Use the discussion board to find other people with whom you are able to network. You might want to find other people who do your job, or other people who are able to spend time online at a particular time, other people who do something different from you - to give you a broader perspective, etc. What is important is that you make an effort to create circles of study buddies who are able to support you in your learning and who you are committed to supporting too.
