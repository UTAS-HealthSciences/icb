Unit Tasks on the MyLO platform
Bachelor of Dementia Care

The Bachelor of Dementia Care is delivered on on the MyLO (My Learning Online) platform. In your units, look out for the following icons that will guide your learning and help you identify different tasks you may be required to do.

 

Assessment Item

 

Assessment Items are important tasks that contribute to your overall grade.

Lecture

 

Your lecturer may engage you via online lectures.

Discussion

 

MyLO Discussion Forums are a place where you can post comments with your peers.

Quiz

 

Online quizzes will test your knowledge.

Reading

 

Your lecturer may prescribe specific readings to support your learning.

Survey

 

You may be asked to participate in surveys.

Activity

 

General tasks and activities will appear here.

Experiment

 

You may be asked to conduct an experimental task.

Reflection

 

Reflection tasks give you the opportunity to think deeply about a topic.

Question

 

You may be asked a specific question.

Thought Tree

 

Thought Trees are a special type of discussion forum where ideas are grown.

Learning Outcomes

 

Learning outcomes are the results and skills you can expect to gain from a unit.

Calendar

 

The calendar will help ou keep track of key dates.
