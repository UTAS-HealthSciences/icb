Interactive Content Builder

Academic Staff Survey

We would appreciate your input

As part of the evaluation of the Interactive Content Builder (ICB) template in MyLO, you are invited to complete a quick (5-10 minute) survey on your experience of the template.

We are only surveying staff with units that have run for a whole semester using the ICB template.

Responses to this survey are anonymous.

We encourage you to provide us with feedback, both so that we can make further improvements to the ICB template, and as input into the overall evidence base for the effectiveness (or otherwise) of the template.

If you would like to help the ICB developers to further develop the ICB, please complete the following short anonymous survey.

You can find out more details about this survey by reading the survey information sheet, which includes details on who to contact if you have any questions, ethics approval details, and how we plan to use the data.

Further infomation can also be found on the ICB website, including FAQs and links to any results or publications that arise from this survey at http://www.utas.edu.au/health/current-staff/learning-and-teaching/interactive-content-builder
