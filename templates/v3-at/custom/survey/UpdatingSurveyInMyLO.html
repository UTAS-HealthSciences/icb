Updating the ICB survey in MyLO

Units that use the ICB have a link to a survey for students about the ICB

By default, each unit that uses the ICB has a survey for students on the impact the ICB has had on their learning experience.

When you initally install the ICB in your unit, this survey is set up to release to students 2 weeks prior to the end of teaching in that semester.

Each semester you will need to update the release dates for that survey and the corresponding news announcement informing students about the survey.

This is relatively simple, it mostly involves just setting a release date on the suvey page/link, the folder it sits in in the table of contents, and the news announcement.

In some units, which have been using the ICB longer, the news announcement may contain a link to the survey page in MyLO. This link often breaks, and we suggest that you edit the news announcement to remove the link, and replace it with equivalent text.

Screenshots of how to do this are included below.
