NOTE: A recent MyLO update prevents ICB Components being pasted into MyLO from this tool.

The MyLO team and ICB template developers are currently working on this issue.

In the meantime, the MyLO Mate plugin for Chrome still allows you to paste ICB components into MyLO pages without a problem.

We therefore recommend that you use Chrome and the MyLO Mate Extension for Chrome to edit your MyLO pages.

Currently MyLO Mate is only available for Chrome. The MyLO Mate team are working on developing versions for other browsers.

To get MyLO Mate for Chrome:

  1. Open (or install) Chrome.
  2. Go to the MyLO Mate website at: https://secure.utas.edu.au/faculty-of-health-intranet/mylo-resources/mylo-mate. Log in with your UTAS username (not email address) and password.
  3. Click the 'available here' link on the MyLO Mate page - this will take you to the MyLO Mate plugin page for Chrome.
  4. Click the 'Add extension' button.

Now whenever you edit a page in MyLO, a panel will appear on the left of your screen from which you can choose ICB components to insert into your page. Just position your cursor on the page where you want to insert a component and browse to the relevant component in the list - click the component to insert it.

Note: MyLO Mate introduces a host of other tweaks to MyLO and MyLO manager, such as:

  • Highlighting in red/green/white units which are past/current/or future units
  • Creating direct links to the assessment [a], turnitin [t], and other tabs or tools in the Assessments tool in MyLO and direct links to particular tools within a unit from MyLO Manager.

You can configure these and many other options by clicking on the MyLO Mate icon at the top right of your Chrome browser (a small 'link' icon that is red when in MyLO or MyLO manager and grey at other times), choosing the 'options' link at the bottom of the panel that opens will allow you to turn on or off each of the MyLO Mate features independently.

Thank you for your patience whilst we troubleshoot this issue with the ICB Componetnts tool, and we hope you enjoy using MyLO Mate!
