Interactive Grouping

The grouping tool allows you to create a game that requires students to divide a single list of objects into two defined groups. Students will drag their selection into one of two group boxes. If they are correct, their selection will highlight green; if incorrect it will highlight red. They can continue until all selections are correct.

When you have copied and pasted the tool into your unit page, insert the titles for your group boxes and list the correct items within each box. You can add extra options by inserting rows (using the MyLO table tool) but DO NOT insert columns, they WILL NOT display correctly.

When the student views the game, all of the options that you have placed within the boxes will be displayed as a randomly ordered list for students to choose from.
