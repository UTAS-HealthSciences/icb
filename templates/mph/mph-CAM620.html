Document Structure

All modules should start with a list of learning objectives.

Please use the Reading, Discussion, Question, and Task boxes as appropriate. These can be copied from below, or from the 'components' file

Please paste reading and reference lists (bibliographies) into a 'Reading' Box a the bottom of your document

After each reference pasted into the reference list at the end of the document please copy the "[Library CAMxxx Reading List]" or "[Library CAMxxx Text List]" link (as appropriate) at the end of the reference. You can copy the links included at bottom of the template page. Don't forget to delete the links form the bottom of the template when you are ready 'publish' your page.

Some notes about pasting EndNote references from MS word into this template:
  • In-text citations inserted into MS word documents that are then pasted into this document should work correctly
  • In Word if one clicks on an in-text citation, the reader is taken to the corresponding citation in the reference/reading list. Currently the corresponding EndNote 'bookmarks' are not copied from Word when references are pasted in to MyLO. We are working on a simple way to solve this problem, but may need to fix these links manually in the short term. Alternatively a student can just scroll to the bottom of the page to find the corresponding reference.
Inserting Echo and YouTube Videos into your document.

The dummy Echo or YouTube video can be copied from the 'components' document into your file. Instructions on how to edit your copy to point to your video are included in that document, along with links to a FAQ page with step-by-step instructions.
