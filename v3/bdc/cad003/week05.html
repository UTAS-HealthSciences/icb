Module 3

This week, writing skills including answering specific questions will be the focus. The lecture focuses on the conceptual stage of discursive essay writing and I encourage you to complete the exercises presented in it with a peer. The focus on constructing a response from a given question is the key to the final essay. There are two readings this week and they are Bailey (2013), titled 'Argument and discussion', as well as a passage from Cottrell 2013, chapter 11. The pages are indicated below. Bailey (2013) adds to the material presented in the lecture. Indeed, he explores the vocabulary and structures needed to present your argument in an appropriate academic manner. Cottrell (2013) gives a wide range of activities for you to interpret and analyse an essay question. Knowing how to do this enables you to construct a response which clearly addresses to question.
