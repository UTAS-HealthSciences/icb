Student Survey:

Unit Template

We would appreciate your input

The online content for this unit has been delivered to you via a new 'template' which basically gives structure to the learning material that you view on each page. The template developers are very keen to receive input on the template so that they can keep improving it. The following survey asks you questions about your impression of the page design (i.e. the background colours, the content boxes and icons etc.), the useability of the template and whether or not you liked the interactive elements (e.g. expanding boxes, sorting games, mini-quizzes etc.).

If you would like to help the template developers to improve the delivery of online courses at the Univeristy of Tasmania, please complete the following short anonymous survey.

You can find out more details about this survey by reading the survey information sheet, which includes details on who to contact if you have any questions, and how we plan to use the data. Survey Information sheet
