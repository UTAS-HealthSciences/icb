Assessment Task 1: Quizzes

Task description Three (3) online multiple choice quizzes relating to ‘the brain’, ‘the diseases’ and ‘the person’ (20 questions each)
Task length Quizzes will be multiple-choice and completed online and thus a word limit is not applicable.
Links to unit’s intended learning outcomes 1, 3
Assessment criteria Correct responses to multiple-choice questions. All quiz questions will be single attempt format and each quiz will have a completion time limit of 20 minutes.
Submission requirements Ensure you formally submit your quizzes through MyLO by the due date.
Contribution to final assessment 20%
Date due 11.59pm, Friday 14th February 2014
